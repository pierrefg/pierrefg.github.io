Music

I have learned music at the Gabriel Fauré music school in Angoulême (France) and have been playing since then:
  • Violin
    • Playing since 2005
    • Teachers: Cécile Mardikian, Manuel Solans, Aude Valensi and Pascal Chiari
    • Diploma: DNOP (National Diploma of Professional Orientation) in 2017
    • Music camps: Académie Musicale de Royan, Académie Musique en Vallée d'Olt, Musicassiopée
    • Orchestras: Gabriel Fauré orchestra, OSIU (Orchestre Symphonique INSA-Université)
    • Some recordings:
      • Kodaly - Duet for violin and cello with Aurèle Guyot-Nessi
      • Tanguy - Elegie for solo violin
      • Mendelssohn - Quatuor op.80, first movement
      • Boccherini - Trio op.6 for two violins and a cello
        • [video]
  • Electroacoustic music
    • From 2008 to 2017
    • Teacher: Edgar Nicouleau
    • Diploma: DNOP in 2017
    • Some of my works:
      • Prelude
      • Eclipse
      • Sablier
      • Decadence
      • Rêverie
...and also classical music theory (Certificate of Musical Studies in 2013), some singing and a bit of jazz drums.
